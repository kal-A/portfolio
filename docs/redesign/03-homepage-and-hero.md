# Homepage Structure and Hero

## Recommended homepage structure

Update the homepage to follow this structure, in order:

1. Hero
2. Selected Case Studies
3. Design Work / Visual Evidence
4. How I Work
5. Project Archive
6. Skills / Ways I Work
7. About
8. Contact

This structure is intentional: the site needs to show strategic product
thinking and design talent early, then back it up with archive depth.

## Hero section

Rewrite the hero to feel less generic and more like Kamal.

**Eyebrow:** Product Design · UX · Systems

**Headline:** I design products for messy real-world workflows.

**Subheadline:** I'm a Management Engineering graduate from the University of
Waterloo. I work across product, UX/UI, operations, and technical systems —
mapping the messy parts first, then turning them into clearer flows,
prototypes, tools, and handoffs.

**CTAs:**
- View case studies
- See resume

The exact copy can be improved, but keep it grounded, human, and specific.

### Avoid in hero copy

- "I build products end to end"
- "from user research to shipped code"
- "0→1"
- "turning ideas into impact"
- generic product buzzwords
