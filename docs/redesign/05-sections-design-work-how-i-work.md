## How I Work section

Add a section called "How I Work" or similar.

Suggested copy:

> I usually start by mapping the workflow before touching the interface.

> Who is involved? Where does the handoff break? What information is
> missing? What is being tracked manually? What does the user actually need
> to finish the task?

> Then I turn that into the product layer: flows, requirements, prototypes,
> dashboards, or implementation details.

Design this as a simple process flow, not a wall of text.

Suggested steps:

1. Map the workflow
2. Find the friction
3. Structure the system
4. Design the experience
5. Prototype, test, or build
6. Document and hand off

This section should reinforce that Kamal is both design-minded and
systems-minded.
